About the Leather

At Inkwali Yenkosi, we take great pride in the quality of the leather we use for our clothing and accessories. Our commitment to excellence starts with the careful selection of the finest materials.

Types of Leather

We offer a range of leather types, each with its own unique characteristics and qualities:

Full-Grain Leather

Full-grain leather is known for its natural grain patterns and durability. It's the top layer of the hide, making it the most authentic and unprocessed form of leather. This type of leather ages beautifully, developing a rich patina over time.

Top-Grain Leather

Top-grain leather is slightly more processed than full-grain but retains many of its qualities. It's smooth, pliable, and resistant to wear and tear. It's often used in high-end leather goods.

Sustainability and Ethics

We are dedicated to ethical and sustainable leather sourcing. Our leather comes from reputable suppliers who adhere to responsible practices. We prioritize eco-friendly tanning methods and support fair labor practices in the leather industry.

Caring for Your Leather

Leather is a resilient material, but it benefits from proper care. To ensure the longevity of your leather items, follow these care tips:

  • Clean leather with a soft, damp cloth.
  • Condition leather regularly to keep it supple.
  • Store leather items in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
